# Red Cap — PvP Guide

> **Faction:** Tuatha Dé Danann
> **Trees:** Class Abilities · Corrupted Blade · Danu's Vengeance · Forest Walking
> **Resource:** Determination (spent on poisons, Earth hits, and detonations) + Aftershock interaction
> **Group Role:** Damage (primary melee assassin / single-target delete)
> **One-line identity:** The detonator — stacks poisons and Aftershock on the kill target, then converts them into an instant burst that deletes a low target inside the team's amp window.

---

## Core Principles

> If you remember nothing else, remember these.

- **Stack first, detonate second.** Your damage isn't in the individual hits — it's in loading poisons and then setting them all off. Deadly Catalyst consumes all Poison DoTs on the target for 75% of their remaining damage *immediately*. Don't detonate before the stacks are built.
- **Three different poisons unlocks the stun.** Flare of Corruption Snares always, but Stuns (1.5/2.5s) only if the target has 3+ *different* Poison effects. Stagger different poison sources (Poison Strike, Poison Dart, Viper's Kiss, Slowing Sap, Poison Weapons) to reach the threshold.
- **You run two detonations: poison and Aftershock.** Final Fracture deals damage equal to the target's Aftershock and removes it; Undermine builds Aftershock (+100% from behind). Attack from behind to load it, then cash it.
- **Bank Determination for the payoff.** Deadly Catalyst (−600) and Final Fracture (−550) are your biggest spends. Don't empty your pool on filler right before a detonation window.
- **The Veil and Stealth are for picks and resets.** Enter Veil, Cut a Path, Death on the Wind, Obscuring Dust, and Fade Into Trees let you reposition, gap-close, and disengage. You choose when the fight starts on you.
- **You die if you're caught in the open.** You're melee with no heavy armor in the data — commit on the team's call, burst, and use a Veil/Stealth tool to reset rather than facetanking.

## Role in the Group

### If Damage
- **Primary job:** Delete the called kill target — per the comp plan, the enemy healer first, then their squishy caster/damage. You're the burst that cashes in the team's amplification (Dark Fool's And Our Hero Falls!, Blessed Crow's Rite of Ruin) and anti-heal (Forest Stalker's Necrotic Toxin, Empath's Comhlacht Mallacht).
- **Positioning:** Flank and backline access — get behind the target (Undermine's +100% Aftershock from behind, and positional safety). Approach from Stealth/Veil or off the Fianna's pull; don't run in through the enemy front.
- **Focus fire:** Commit on the team's target call. Load poisons + Aftershock during the setup, then detonate (Deadly Catalyst / Final Fracture) inside the called burst window. Swap targets only if the kill is secured or the window is dead.
- **Peel responsibility:** Light — your snares/roots (Slowing Sap, Shackles of Stone, Flare of Corruption) can briefly peel a diver off your healers, but your job is offense; hand sustained peel to the Fianna.
- **What you do NOT do:** Don't tunnel a tank — you're for squishies. Don't detonate Deadly Catalyst before the poison stacks are loaded. Don't dive without a reset (Cut a Path / Obscuring Dust available). Don't blow your burst into the enemy's defensive window — wait for the team's amp.

Then detonate. ## Pre-Engage

- **Poison Weapons** — adds 50 Poison damage to all your Direct damage; press before contact so every hit seeds poison.
- **Poison Mastery** — +10% poison damage dealt; stage it as a standing buff.
- **Enter Veil / Fade Into Trees** — pre-position in the Veil or Stealth so you open from an angle the enemy can't track (Fade Into Trees breaks on move/ability — use it to lurk, not to travel).
- **Deflecting Stones** — negate the next 3 Elemental hits (30s) if you expect elemental burst on approach.

[Approach: Cut a Path (brief Veil + big move speed) or Death on the Wind (leap from the Veil) to close, or come off the Fianna's Inescapable Vines pull.]

## Engage / Opener

> Two opener modes: the stealth/Veil pick and the off-pull dive.

**Mode A — From Stealth / the Veil:**
1. Death on the Wind — leap from the Veil to the target (purpose: instant gap close, only works Veil→Normal-plane target).
2. Undermine (from behind) — heavy Earth hit that loads Aftershock at +100% (purpose: start the Aftershock detonation setup).
3. Poison Strike → Poison Dart → Viper's Kiss — stack different poisons toward the 3-poison threshold.

**Mode B — Off the Fianna's pull:**
1. Poison Strike (×up to 3) + Poison Dart — seed poisons fast on the pulled target.
2. Flare of Corruption — Snare, and Stun if 3+ different poisons are already up.
3. Undermine — load Aftershock for Final Fracture.

**Opener goal:** Target loaded with multiple poison stacks and Aftershock, Snared/Stunned, and you positioned behind them — ready to detonate the moment the team's amp window opens.

## Core Rotation

**Maintain:**
- Poison stacks on the kill target — keep Poison Strike (stacks up to 3) and your other poisons refreshed
- Poison Weapons buff up so every Direct hit adds poison
- Aftershock building via Undermine (from behind) toward Final Fracture / the team's Fracture

**Loop:**
- Apply/refresh poisons with Poison Strike, Poison Dart, Viper's Kiss, Slowing Sap
- Build Earth/Aftershock with Undermine, Stone Fang, Earthen Daggers
- Caress of Death as a filler that scales +30% per poison stack (up to +90%) — strong once stacks are high
- Keep Determination banked toward a detonation

**Burst window — detonate (on the team's call):**
- Deadly Catalyst → consumes all Poison DoTs for 75% of remaining damage instantly; and/or Final Fracture → detonates Aftershock. Fire these inside the Dark Fool's And Our Hero Falls! / Death by a Thousand Cuts amp.

## Burst / Execute Phase

> When the target is low or the team's amp is up, detonate everything.

1. **Deadly Catalyst** — consumes all Poison DoTs on the target for 75% of their remaining damage immediately. This is your primary execute — make sure the stacks are loaded first, so you're cashing a full bank, not an empty one.
2. **Caress of Death** — +30% per poison stack (up to +90%); use it *before* Deadly Catalyst consumes the stacks if you want the stack-scaled hit too.
3. **Final Fracture** — detonate the Aftershock you (and the Fianna / Forest Stalker) built, for damage equal to the stored amount.
4. **Flare of Corruption** — if the target is at 3+ poisons, the Stun (1.5/2.5s) locks them for the team to finish.

*Sequencing note (inference):* Caress of Death scales with the poison stacks and Deadly Catalyst consumes them, so reading the text, you generally want the stack-scaled hit before the detonation that removes the stacks — but the database doesn't specify ordering rules, so confirm the timing in practice.

## Defensive Layer / Survival

**Immediate response:**
1. Dance of Death — +50% Parry for 8s and damage to attackers when you Parry (turn being focused into punishment)
2. Whirling Leaves — 50% chance to avoid Ranged Physical hits for 10s (vs archers)
3. Deflecting Stones — negate the next 3 Elemental hits (vs casters)

**Reinforce / mitigate:**
- Trained Tolerance — removes all DoTs from you and heals you (your self-cleanse; great vs enemy poison/DoT)

**If still losing the exchange:**
- Obscuring Dust — removes you from Combat and Stealths you for 15s (hard reset)
- Cut a Path — brief Veil + big move speed to disengage
- Enter Veil — shift planes to break the train

**The rule:** You don't out-tank damage — you leave. If the burst didn't kill and you're being focused, reset with Obscuring Dust / Veil / Cut a Path and re-enter on your terms rather than trading to the death.

## Break CC

> Your "break" is really avoidance and a self-cleanse — be honest the database shows no hard CC-break.

**You are soft CC'd (rooted/snared):**
- Trained Tolerance removes all DoTs (and the poison-snares that come with them) and heals you — your best answer to poison/DoT-based slows. Cut a Path's move-speed surge also helps shake a snare once you can move.

**You are hard CC'd (stunned):**
- No innate hard-CC break in the data. Avoid it: open from Stealth/Veil and stay behind the target so you're harder to pin. Lean on the Empath's coverage and the Fianna's peel.

**Priority rule:** Trained Tolerance answers DoT/poison-snare; for hard CC you rely on positioning and the team. Don't expect to power through a stun — plan not to be in front of the enemy's stun in the first place.

**After breaking / resetting:** Re-stealth or Veil, reposition behind the target, and reload poisons + Aftershock before re-committing.

## Peel / Protecting the Backline

> Secondary duty — you're offense first, but you can buy the healers a moment.

- **Slowing Sap** — Snare (20%, 6s) + poison on a diver hitting your backline.
- **Flare of Corruption** — Snare, and a Stun if the diver already has 3+ poisons.
- **Shackles of Stone** — Root (3/6s) *if* the target has ≥1000 Aftershock; situational, only if you've loaded Aftershock on that diver.

**Peel priority:** Slowing Sap or Flare of Corruption to slow/stun the diver, then disengage straight back to your kill target — sustained peel is the Fianna's job, not yours. Only commit Shackles of Stone if the diver happens to be Aftershock-loaded.

## Target Priority / Matchups

You hunt the called kill target — the **enemy healer** first per the comp plan, then their squishy caster/damage. You want to land *after* the anti-heal (Necrotic Toxin / Comhlacht Mallacht) and *inside* the amp (And Our Hero Falls! / Rite of Ruin) so your detonation isn't healed back. Get behind the target for Undermine's bonus Aftershock and positional safety. Against kite/ranged enemies, use Cut a Path / Death on the Wind to stick and Whirling Leaves to survive the poke — but if they're truly running, let the Fianna pull and the Forest Stalker pressure while you wait for the lockdown. Avoid diving into the enemy's defensive cooldowns; coordinate your detonation with the team's call.

## Class-Specific Mechanics

- **Poison stacking + detonation.** Poison Strike stacks up to 3; Poison Weapons adds poison to every Direct hit; Poison Mastery / Distilled-style buffs increase it. Flare of Corruption keys off 3+ *different* poisons for its Stun. Deadly Catalyst consumes all Poison DoTs for 75% of remaining damage at once — the more you've loaded, the bigger the detonation.
- **Aftershock (cross-class Earth system).** Undermine builds Aftershock (+100% from behind); Final Fracture, Shackles of Stone (root at ≥1000 Aftershock), and Blinding Grit (disarm if Aftershock present) consume or key off it. You're the team's main Aftershock detonator alongside the Fianna (Nature's Sundering) and Forest Stalker. *Inference:* the text describes Aftershock as a value "on the target," so the Fianna's and Forest Stalker's Earth hits plausibly add to the same pool you detonate — treat cross-class pooling as a likely synergy to coordinate, not a stated guarantee.
- **The Veil (plane-shifting).** Enter Veil shifts you to the Veil; Death on the Wind leaps from the Veil to a Normal-plane target; Cut a Path dips into the Veil for a speed burst. Note: Forest Stalker's Mark Target *prevents* enemies entering the Veil — the enemy may have the same tool, so don't rely on the Veil against a target that's Marked you.
